  The advantage of the time-frequency diagram is that it can analyze the frequency characteristics of more complex signals and help understand the details of the sound.
  For example, what instrument was used in that section. And the intensity of frequency energy can be observed from the distribution of colors. Red represents high-energy frequencies, and green to blue represent low-energy frequencies. 
  It can also show the amplitude changes in volume, for example, the high amplitude area corresponds to the louder volume, representing the human voice. The waveform is more intuitive and easier to understand, and it can determine whether the volume is uniform but it can only show the changes in volume. 
  It is also impossible to distinguish specific instruments and sounds. This is not an advantage when analyzing synthesis or large symphonies.
