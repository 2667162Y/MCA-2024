Transcription Analysis

    In the transcribed version the new tune sounds more elegant and complex. But in the transcribed version there are some important elements missing that have been tampered with. 
    For example, the name of the composer and the tune and the beat are missing. The original upgrade symbols have been changed. 
    Listening to the piece as a whole, one can hear the similarities with the original, some important chords are still there, and the rhythm of the notes as a whole seems to be in harmony, but there are major changes in the details. 
    Finally, looking at the entire score, the pages look a bit confusing and unattractive, and will need to be manually modified at a later stage to make it look easy to read.
